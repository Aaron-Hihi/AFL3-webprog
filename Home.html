All About Me

Hi, I'm Aaron Hans Adiputra, a passionate individual eager to about the world. I'm 18 years old, currently studying informatics in Universitas Ciputra on 2nd semester.

Photography and programming are two of my greatest passions, each offering a unique way to explore and create. Photography allows me to capture beautiful moments into timeless images, whether it's the intensity of a sports event or the serenity of a landscape.

On the other hand, programming allows me to solve problems and bring my ideas to life in a digital space. The creative process of writing code, much like photography, is about precision, patience, and continuous learning.

Outside of photography, I enjoy exploring different aspects of life, whether it's cooking up new dishes, inventing melodies through piano, or editing incredible videos. I believe in always striving to learn to grow in both my personal and professional life.
